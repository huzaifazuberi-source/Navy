About This Project

Understanding the Pakistan Navy Eligibility Predictor.

Project Overview

An academic eligibility predictor

This portal is an academic project that predicts whether a candidate is likely to be eligible for Pakistan Navy recruitment. It studies the candidate's age, physical fitness, medical status, education and skills using a trained machine learning model.

The aim is to help candidates understand their standing and prepare better before applying through official channels.

  • Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Flask.
  • Machine learning powered by Scikit-Learn.
  • Records stored in a PostgreSQL (Supabase) database.

Important Notice

This is a guidance and learning tool only. The prediction is an estimate and does not represent an official decision. Final selection is made solely by the Pakistan Navy through its official recruitment process.

What You Get

Your Detailed Report Includes

Eligibility & Confidence

A breakdown of where you are strong and where you can improve.
