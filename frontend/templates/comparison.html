What the metrics mean

  • Accuracy — how many predictions were correct overall.
  • Precision — of those predicted Eligible, how many truly were.
  • Recall — of all truly eligible candidates, how many were found.
  • F1 Score — a balance between precision and recall.
  • CV Accuracy — average accuracy across 5 cross-validation folds (used to pick the best model).
