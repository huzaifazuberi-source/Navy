The Eligibility Predictor

How the predictor works

The predictor uses a trained machine learning model to study your age, physical fitness, education, medical status and skills, then estimates your eligibility for Pakistan Navy recruitment.

  • Fill a simple candidate form with your details.
  • The model analyses your complete profile.
  • Receive a full report in seconds.
  • Save and review your prediction history any time.
